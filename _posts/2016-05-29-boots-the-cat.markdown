One day a momma cat was taking a stroll down the alley.
Her five little kittens were all snug in their bed taking a nap.

As she was walking along she heard a disturbing noise, and she decided to check it out. 

In an empty lot something was crying for momma.
As she reached the spot she found a tiny ball of white fur that was hungry and crying.

The momma cat wondered where this furball's momma was.
She decided to sit by it until it's momma came back.
She gave it a bath and some milk to drink.
She laid beside it to keep it warm.

It was getting late and the momma cat needed to get back to her kittens.
The furball's momma was nowhere to be found so she picked it up and brought it home.

The next day the people came to check on the momma cat and her kittens.
They were surprised to see a new kitten, only when they picked it up they saw it was a puppy!

They picked him up and saw that he was all white except his legs were brown from his paws to the middle of his legs.

"We'll call him Boots." the woman said.
"Boots the cat!" exclaimed the man. "What an interesting cat he'll be."

The momma cat took care of Boots like her other five kittens.
Now she had six little babies that she was proud to call her own.
