I've seen signs around towns before for an organization called the Optimist Club.
I'm not really sure what the Optimist Club is or what they do, but I really think they need a rival.
So I'm proposing the Pessimist Club.

We'd be equal yet opposite, the yin to their yang, the negative side of the magnet. Basically we'd balance each other out.

Imagine the softball games we could play (or lose). 

I've often thought about actually setting up a Pessimist Club, but never have because I figured no one would join. Plus it's probably a lot of work.
